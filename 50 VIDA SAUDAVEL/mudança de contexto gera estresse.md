This pastor also drew on diverse skill sets throughout the day. Studies have shown that toggling between tasks that involve different skill sets can result in a particular kind of stressor termed “task switching.” In fact, toggling from task to task can be harder on someone than doing the same very stressful task all day—because the very act of changing gears costs mental resources and can slow a person down and lead to errors. (p.2) 

- Mudança constante de contexto, aplicando skills diferentes a cada mudança, gera [[estresse]]. Até um termos foi criado: task switching
- Os autores afirmam que é melhor uma só tarefa, ainda que muito estressante, do que várias tarefas com mudanças de contexto durante o dia
